Security for lawyers

Built for professional secrecy.

Brieflee is designed to meet the confidentiality standards lawyers are held to. Your client data is never used for AI training, always encrypted, and hosted entirely in the EU.
EU hosted GDPR compliant Azure OpenAI ISO 27001 (in progress)
What this protects you from. Brieflee is built from the ground up around the security concerns that matter most to legal professionals.
Breach of confidentiality
Client data stays in your workspace. It is never shared across accounts, never visible to other users, and never used outside your session.
AI model training on your data
Your documents and queries are never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI model. This is contractually guaranteed by our Azure OpenAI agreement.
GDPR non-compliance
All data is processed and stored in the EU (West Europe). No data is transferred to the US or any non-EU country. You remain the data controller at all times.
Unauthorized access
Access is protected by secure authentication, session management, and strict workspace isolation. Each account is fully separated at the database level.
Opposing counsel or third-party access
There is no shared data layer. Workspaces are isolated by design. No user can access, search, or reference another user's data under any circumstance.
Data leaving the EU
All infrastructure, including AI processing, runs in the EU on Microsoft Azure. No data is transferred to or stored in non-EU countries.

Questions lawyers ask us

Can opposing counsel access my research or documents?
No. Every workspace is fully isolated. There is no shared database, no cross-account search, and no way for any user to access another user's data. Your research, documents, and outputs are visible only to you.
Is my client data used to train AI models?
No. We use Azure OpenAI, which contractually guarantees that your data is never used for model training, fine-tuning, or improvement. Your inputs and outputs are processed in real time and not retained by the AI provider.
Where is my data stored?
All data is hosted in the EU (West Europe region on Microsoft Azure). This includes application data, uploaded documents, database storage, and AI processing. No data is transferred outside the EU.
Can Brieflee employees see my documents?
Brieflee staff do not have routine access to customer data. Access to production systems is restricted, logged, and only permitted for critical operational reasons with appropriate authorization.
Is Brieflee compatible with professional secrecy obligations?
Yes. Brieflee is designed to align with the confidentiality obligations of the legal profession. Data isolation, EU hosting, no AI training, encryption at rest and in transit, and strict access controls all support compliance with professional secrecy standards.

Technical controls
Who can access your workspace
Technical controls that protect your account and data at every layer.
Authentication
Secure email/password login with hashed credentials. Social login via trusted OAuth providers.
Access control
Role-based permissions within workspaces. Only authorised users can access account data.
Data isolation
Every workspace is logically isolated at the database level. No data is shared between accounts.
Encryption in transit
All traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.2+. API calls, file uploads, and AI requests are all protected.
Encryption at rest
All stored data, including database records and uploaded files, is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
Backups
Automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery. Backups are stored encrypted in the EU.
Monitoring
24/7 infrastructure monitoring with alerting for anomalies, errors, and potential security events.
Vulnerability management
Regular dependency updates, automated scanning, and secure development practices across the stack.

How AI is used. Brieflee uses AI to help lawyers research, analyse, and draft. Here is exactly what that means for your data.
  • Your data is never used to train any AI model.
  • Your inputs and outputs are not retained by the AI provider after processing.
  • All AI processing happens in the EU (West Europe) via Azure OpenAI.
  • AI requests are transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS 1.2+).
  • Only your session data is sent to the model. No cross-account data is ever included.
Private AI processing via Azure OpenAI
Brieflee uses Azure OpenAI Service, which provides a private, enterprise-grade AI endpoint. Unlike consumer AI products, Azure OpenAI contractually guarantees that customer data is not used for model training or improvement, and is not accessible to other customers or to OpenAI.
AI-specific questions
Does OpenAI have access to my data?
No. We use Azure OpenAI, which is a separate service from OpenAI's consumer products. Your data is processed within Microsoft's infrastructure under enterprise data protection agreements. OpenAI does not have access to your inputs or outputs.
Is my data stored after an AI request?
No. Azure OpenAI does not store your prompts or completions after processing. Data is used only for the duration of the API call and is not retained.
Can AI outputs from one user influence another user?
No. Each request is independent. The model does not learn from, remember, or share data across users or sessions. There is no cross-contamination of data between accounts.
What data is sent to the AI model?
Only the data required for your specific request is sent: your query, relevant document excerpts, and context from your current session. No data from other users or accounts is ever included.
Is the AI connection encrypted?
Yes. All communication between Brieflee and Azure OpenAI is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Data is protected both in transit and at rest.
