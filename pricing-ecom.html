FAQ
What is Brieflee?
Brieflee is a legal research and drafting platform that helps lawyers search case law, analyze documents, and draft legal outputs with verifiable sources.
What can I do with Brieflee?
You can use Brieflee to:
- Search Belgian and EU case law and articles of law using both keyword search and natural language questions
- Verify legal answers with citations and jump to the exact source text
- Upload your own documents to analyze them in context
- Identify legal issues, weaknesses, and counter-arguments
- Structure timelines and key facts across multiple documents
- Draft legal documents and conclusions that you can edit and refine

Brieflee supports legal research, analysis, and drafting, while keeping the lawyer in control.
Who is Brieflee for?
Brieflee is designed for lawyers and legal professionals who need reliable legal research, source-backed answers, and drafting support for real cases.
How is Brieflee different from a general AI chatbot?
Brieflee is purpose-built for legal work — not a general-purpose chatbot. Every output is grounded in case law, statutes, and verified legal sources. You can inspect each citation, view the full source text, and verify the reasoning before relying on it.
Can I upload my own documents?
Yes. You can upload contracts, pleadings, judgments, and other legal documents to analyze them in context. Brieflee can extract timelines, identify issues, and generate draft outputs grounded in your files.
Can I verify where an answer comes from?
Yes. Brieflee shows citations and allows you to view the exact passage in the source document (“view in full text”) behind each legal statement.
Does Brieflee replace legal judgment or advice?
No. Brieflee is a tool that supports research, analysis, and drafting. The lawyer always remains in control — all legal judgment and final responsibility stay with you.
Is my client data used to train AI models?
No. Your documents, queries, and outputs are never used to train any AI model — not ours, and not any third party’s.
Where is my data hosted?
All data is hosted in the EU (AWS, Paris region) and encrypted at rest and in transit. AI capabilities are delivered through Azure OpenAI, with no data shared outside your secure environment.
